Ada & Zangemann

A Tale of Software, Skateboards, and Raspberry Ice Cream

Zangemann is a world-famous and immensely wealthy inventor. Children and adults alike love his fabulous inventions. But suddenly, big trouble : the kids' electronic skateboards bug and the ice creams all have the same flavor. What's going on ?

Ada, a curious young girl, is about to discover how Zangemann controls his products from his golden computer. Together with her friends, she will tinker with computerized objects that escape Zangemann's decisions.

A book for children and young teenagers that could well teach them the joy of tinkering. A book about free computing, camaraderie and the role of girls in promoting technology for autonomy. A lively, superbly illustrated tale.
