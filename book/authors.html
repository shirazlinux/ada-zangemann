About the author Matthias Kirschner

Close-up of Matthias smiling, waving with one hand, and wearing a headset. Matthias has short hair and a little beard. (fin de la description)

Matthias was fascinated by computers and software as a child. He had an internet connection early, through which he learned a lot. Children have a right to take their future into their own hands. Therefore, Matthias is committed to the goal that all humans can use software in a self-determined way. He also wants to build an electric skateboard with Free Software.
